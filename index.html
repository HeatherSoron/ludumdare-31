You, Color-o, live in a world composed of 3 planes (or "colors") of existence: the Red, the Green, and the Blue.
Most things in this world can only interact with their own color. However, you have the special ability to shift between different colors. You have also heard of the mystical Several Stars, objects of power that belong - somehow! - to two separate colors at once.
Just beware of the terrifying Black, which can absorb power from any color!

Placing platforms takes 10 power each.
Attacking takes 0.5 power each time.
Super attacks take 5 power, but are black.
Only normal attacks can be used when power is low.
Killing an enemy gives 10 power of each OTHER color.

Controls:

  • left/right (arrow keys) to move
  • space to jump
  • 1, Z, 2, X, 3, C, or up/down to shift color
  • Q, W, E to place colored platforms
  • A, S, D for a power ball (attack)
  • F for a super-attack (black!)

Tips:

  • Kill ALL the enemies - killing enemies refills your power bars
  • When your current color's power is low, you're vulnerable! Switch to a color where you have lots of power, instead.
  • Picking up the Several Stars will increase your maximum power
