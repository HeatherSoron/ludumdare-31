Placing platforms takes 10 power each.
Attacking takes 0.5 power each time.
Super attacks take 5 power, but are black.
Only normal attacks can be used when power is low.
Killing an enemy gives 10 power of each OTHER color.

Controls:

  • left/right (arrow keys) to move
  • space to jump
  • up/down to shift color
  • q, w, e to place colored platforms
  • a, s, d to attack
  • f for a super-attack (black!)

Tips:

  • Kill ALL the enemies - killing enemies refills your power bars
  • When your current color's power is low,
    you're vulnerable!
    Switch to a color where you have lots of power
  • After you've gained a power-up star,
    your gain double power in a corresponding color
